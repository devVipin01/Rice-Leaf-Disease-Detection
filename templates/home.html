What is Image Processing?

Image processing and disease detection in leaves are two of the most researched areas. Researchers are racing to detect plant diseases early so that they can be treated. India is more reliant on crops such as wheat and rice. Rice is the most consumed crop in India. The main factor causing high-quality and quantity losses in agricultural manufacturing is plant diseases. Both the volume of production and the farmer's income are decreased by these losses. To ensure the healthful and proper growth of the rice vegetation, it is critical in the agricultural industry to detect any disorder in time and prior to treating the bothered plants.

The purpose of predicting rice leaf diseases for farmers is to facilitate healthy growth of rice plants and ensure adequate supply and food security to the rapidly increasing population. Early diagnosis and appropriate remedy of rice leaf infection is crucial for this. Rice leaf diseases often pose threats to the sustainable production of rice, affecting many farmers around the world. For disease management, farmers spend a lot of time and resources detecting diseases through their naked eye approach which leads to unhealthy farming. The advancement of technical support in agriculture greatly assists for automatic identification of infectious organisms in rice plants leaves.
